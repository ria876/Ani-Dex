Animal Type:/:Crocodile
Species/Breed:/:Some crocodile species are Saltwater crocodile, Nile crocodile, American crocodile, Freshwater crocodile, New Guinea crocodile, etc. 
Life Span:/:20 - 70 yrs
Locations:/:Found in Africa, North, Central and South America, Oceania, Asia. Mostly in Temperate to Tropical zones. Around Rivers, Lakes, marshes, lagoons, mangrove swamps and estuaries
Population Size:/:Species Dependent
Lifestyle:/:As Carnivores that prey on smaller fish, crustaceans, deer and others of the like. Crocodiles are social among their peers having hierarchies and group feeding behavior. Like most reptiles they are unable to regulate their body temperature themselves and instead rely on their environment (mostly sun). They make hissing sounds to threaten other creatures.
