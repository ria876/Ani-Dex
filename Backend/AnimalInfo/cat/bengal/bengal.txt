Animal Type:/:Cat
Species/Breed:/:Bengal Cats
Lifespan:/: 12-16 years
Appearance:/:Distinctive spotted or marbled coat resembling that of a wild leopard or ocelot. Typically have a sleek, muscular build with large eyes and prominent cheekbones.
Personality:/:Active, playful, and highly intelligent. Enjoys climbing, playing fetch, and interactive toys. Often more vocal than other breeds and may form strong bonds with their owners.
Health:/:Generally healthy but can be prone to certain genetic conditions like progressive retinal atrophy (PRA) and hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM).
Care:/:Require regular playtime and mental stimulation to prevent boredom. Need occasional grooming to maintain their short coat.
