Animal Type: Bird
Species/Breed:/: Red-Tailed Hawk (Buteo jamaicensis)
Lifespan:/: 21 years
Locations:/: The Red-tailed hawk breeds throughout most of North America. It is one of the most common members within the genus of Buteo in North America or worldwide.
Population:/: According to AllAboutBirds, an estimated 2.3 million.
Lifestyle:/: Diurnal, Soaring Bird, Ambush Predator, Solitary, Partial Migrant, Territorial. Red-tailed hawks are carnivores, they eat small mammals including voles, rats, ground squirrels and rabbits, birds, and reptiles, especially snakes, bats, frogs, toads, and insects. They may feed on carrion.
