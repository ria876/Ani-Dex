Lifestyle:/:The cheetah is a carnivorous animal whose diet contains Gazelle, Wildebeest, Hare, etc. Unlike other African felines, cheetahs are primarily daytime creatures. This allows them to avoid competition from other felines or hyenas. They use stealth as their main weapon for hunting prey and in spite of their shy nature they are fiercely territorial.
